Mac the Cat

Birth of a legend

15 October 2017

I was born in Walsall, England. My parents are blue ragdolls. I have two siblings. My mummy brought me up along with her lady partner. My daddy lives else where.

Adoption Day

6 January 2018

One morning a new hooman came. She put me in a carrier box and drove somewhere. I cried all the way there. When we arrived I was in my new home. The new hooman introduced me to her partner. It was very scary but they made me feel loved. They got me all these gifts and played with me and gave me lots of cuddles.

My Hoomans

My favourite hoomans in the world.

My favourite fooman is called Dominika. She looks after me and makes sure that I have everything I need. My other hooman is called Gareth. He has a thick dressing gown and lets me kneed on it.

Adventure Day

The day I tried to run away.

One day my favourite hooman allowed me to hang out in the back garden by myself. I decided to explore the world behind the fence. It was not particulary exciting. I got lost. I thought I would never find my way home but then I heard my favourite hooman's voice. I cried until she found me and took me back. I will never go exploring again.

First Birthday

Chicken day.

One afternoon my hoomans gave me lots of chicken and wished me happy birthday. I am still unsure exactly what it means but I hope it happens again soon. I love chicken.
